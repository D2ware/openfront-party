OpenFront Party Companion

Your match data stays on the party route.

This policy explains the limited information processed when you explicitly link the companion to an OpenFront party.

Linked by youUnlink any time

Who operates the service

OpenFront Party Companion and its party relay are operated by Nonekode. Privacy and deletion requests can be sent to nijisafari@gmail.com.

Information we process

  • Your party callsign, random member identifier, random companion credential, party membership and Ready/game state.
  • Selected lobby identifiers and the navigation state needed to keep the party together.
  • A finalized match summary: game and map identifiers, result, territory, attacks, donated troops and gold, buildings, nuclear weapons and generated gold.
  • Standard connection metadata, such as IP address and request time, may be processed temporarily by hosting and security infrastructure.

Information we do not collect

The companion does not read or transmit your OpenFront password, cookies, account or play tokens, Turnstile responses, chat, complete browsing history, or raw WebSocket and game-worker streams.

Why and when data is used

The random credential and current state operate party launches and persistence. Finalized summaries produce the Match history requested by party members. Processing starts only after you choose Agree and link OpenFront. It stops when you unlink, leave the party or remove the extension.

Storage and retention

The extension stores its credential, settings and up to 20 summaries locally in your browser. The relay stores at most the latest 200 party match records; older records are displaced by newer reports. Temporary party state is removed after the party becomes inactive. The service does not sell data or use it for advertising.

Sharing and security

Linked data is sent over HTTPS only to moss.nonekode.fi. Public Match history can display party callsigns and finalized match statistics. Random bearer credentials are stored as hashes on the relay. Data is not shared with advertisers or data brokers.

Your choices

Linking is optional. Use Unlink in the companion panel to stop the connection, remove the extension to erase its local storage, or contact us to request deletion of a server-side match report. You may continue using the lobby board without linking the companion.

Changes

Material changes to collected data or its purpose will be disclosed before the updated companion begins that processing. This policy is effective 14 July 2026.
